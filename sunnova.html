--- layout: sunnova title: Sunnova Catalyst about: Sunnova's main service offering is financing for solar panel systems for residential homes; and they use mobile apps to help accomplish this-- which is where my software development skillsets come in. The app I helped build in 2023 (with a team of ~15 people) is called 'Sunnova Catalyst' on the app stores. Catalyst is a business-to-franchise app for Sunnova authorized solar panel dealerships to use with residential solar panel installation. After dealership employees install Sunnova-approved equipment on houses they submit photos of the installed equipment to Sunnova, and then Sunnova sends them a commissioning check after verifying that the equipment is installed correctly through a review website. Throughout this process the consumers also are following along in a "install wizard" through the consumer facing app (after they pass the underwriting process), and once their equipment is installed they can use the app to monitor/customize their residential home energy systems. about1: The app I spent about a year working on updated the photo submission process for solar panel dealers and the Sunnova infrastructure (photo) reviewers, and it is actually the third time Sunnova has updated this submission process. The first was dealers sending emails with photos to Sunnova, the second was a Xamarin-based (Microsoft's cross-platform mobile development framework) app that allowed single PDF uploading, and this latest app I built is their new update to the process— a React native and typescript app. This newest version of the commissioning app/process has custom survey logic that supports photo-uploading, comments, and approving/rejecting. The codebase also has a website for Sunnova´s electrical engineering reviewers that allows them to leave comments on why a commissioning package was rejected, and the rejection details + history are saved in Salesforce for Sunnova corporate staff to monitor and assist when needed. about2: The purpose of this website is to display what I built while at Sunnova because it was an innovative businesses and I enjoyed my time,... Features: The codebase for Catalyst supports an ios and android app for solar-dealerships to submit (solar installation) 'site-survey's' and 'commissioning packages' to Sunnova for review so that the solar-dealerships can get a commissioning-payout from Sunnova. There also is a web app for Sunnova-corporate-staff/engineers to review site-survey's + commissioning packages and approve/reject them. All these apps use Salesforce to store data and communicate with each other. The main purpose of all these embedded apps inside/connecting other apps is to help dealerships (with Sunnova as the Franchise owner) sell + install solar panel systems and accessories. siteSurvey1: Before Sunnova can quote a customer with how much installing solar equipment on their property will cost they need to figure out some specifications of the property. ie; roof pitch, attic + roof rafter specs, subpanel + main breaker, what electrical utility company they are using, etc, etc. This portion of thier app is called "site survey", and it is a survey that dealers fill out, and then the data is saved in postgres Sql tables. Then the data from these SQL tables is ported over to Salesforce via Mulesoft.
